# Hi, I'm Anu 👋

I'm a computational biologist and ML engineer with a PhD in Neuroscience (University of Göttingen). My background sits at the intersection of hands-on preclinical research and data-driven engineering. I've spent years both collecting biological data in the lab and building the pipelines and models to make sense of it.

I'm interested in roles at the intersection of biology and computation, in industry and research alike, particularly in biomedical and life science applications.

---

## What I Build

**Biosignal ML** : 1D CNNs for automated detection and latency estimation of auditory brainstem response peaks in preclinical data. Trained on real lab data, evaluated with precision/recall metrics and sub-millisecond regression error.

**Data Engineering** : ETL pipelines collecting data from REST APIs and web scraping, loading into relational SQL databases, deployed and automated on Google Cloud Platform.

**LLM Applications** : RAG-based research assistant for querying large PDF collections using LlamaIndex, MMR retrieval, and Groq LLM. Handles incremental indexing across hundreds of documents.

**Computational Imaging** : Interactive GUI tools for large-scale 3D crop generation from light-sheet microscopy datasets, used in deep learning segmentation workflows across a research lab.
